    About me

    Right now, my work mainly goes in two directions. One is about processing subbottom profiler data — things like denoising, signature detecting, and horizon picking. Lately, I’ve been especially focused on identifying those hyperbola-shaped signatures that show up in the data. The other direction is more on the science side, looking into scientific problems related to back-arc basins. At the moment, my main study area is the Parece Vela Basin.

    Outside of research, I’m into World Heritage hunting, TCG, sneakers, Liverpool FC, and playing basketball casually. I get excited whenever there’s something new and unknown to explore.
