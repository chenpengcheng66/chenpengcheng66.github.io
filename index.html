    About me

    I received the B.S. degree in electronic information science and technology and the M.E. degree in software engineering from Southwest Petroleum University (SWPU), Chengdu, China, in 2015 and 2020, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in geophysics from Sun Yat-sen University, Zhuhai, China, in 2025.
    I worked as a Research Associate with the School of Resources and Environment, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, in 2021. I am currently a Lecturer with the School of Computer Science and Software Engineering, SWPU.
    My research interests include high-performance computing, acoustic signal analysis, and image processing for geophysics.
